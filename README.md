# BlankMap Mobile

### Fill in the blanks of your city.

## Introduction

BlankMap is a community-driven mapping platform that helps people
discover essential public facilities in their city. The platform allows
users to contribute and share information about public services that are
often missing or hard to find on traditional maps.

Instead of relying only on centralized data, BlankMap empowers citizens
to collaboratively build a useful and inclusive map of their
environment.

## Problem Statement

In many cities, people struggle to find basic public facilities such as
toilets, drinking water points, dustbins, or accessibility services.
Existing mapping platforms mainly focus on navigation and businesses,
leaving many essential civic services unmapped or outdated.

This lack of accessible information leads to inconvenience, poor
sanitation, and difficulty for travelers, elderly people, and people
with disabilities.

## Why This Problem Matters

Cities should be accessible and easy to navigate for everyone. When
people cannot locate essential facilities, it affects public health,
cleanliness, safety, and overall convenience.

More importantly, cities work best when communities actively share
knowledge and support each other. A collaborative mapping system enables
citizens to contribute information about their surroundings, creating a
living community-driven map that improves urban life.

## Proposed Solution

BlankMap provides a crowdsourced platform where users can discover and
add locations of important public facilities.

Users can contribute information and help maintain an updated map that
benefits everyone. The goal is to create a community-powered civic
infrastructure map.

## Features

Users can map essential public facilities such as:

-   🚻 Public Toilets
-   🚰 Drinking Water Points
-   🚮 Public Dustbins
-   🚌 Bus Stops and Transport Points
-   👶 Women & Infant Care Facilities (Breastfeeding / Nursing Rooms)
-   ♿ Accessibility Facilities (Ramps, Wheelchair Access, Accessible
    Toilets)

Additional features include:

-   Community-based facility updates
-   Location-based search for nearby services
-   User contributions to add new facilities
-   Reporting or updating facility conditions

## How the Solution Works

1.  Users open the platform and view a map interface showing nearby
    public facilities.
2.  They can search for essential services like toilets, water points,
    or accessibility facilities.
3.  If a facility is missing, users can add the location with details
    and photos.
4.  Other users can verify, update, or report the condition of the
    facility.

Over time, this creates a collaborative and continuously improving civic
map.

## Impact

BlankMap aims to:

-   Improve access to essential public facilities
-   Support inclusive urban environments
-   Encourage community participation in city improvement
-   Help citizens and travelers easily find important services

## Future Enhancements

-   Facility rating and cleanliness score
-   AI-based detection of areas lacking essential services
-   Real-time updates and notifications
-   Integration with smart city infrastructure
